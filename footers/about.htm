How does Trust Accounts work?

Trust Accounts acts as a bridge that connects to individual blockchains that is integrated to it. Every blockchain has their own set of public addresses. These addresses are where the Crypto are encrypted and stored. Trust Accounts does not hold or control any Crypto, it just gives the user access to it if they own the correct Private Keys or Recovery Phrase. Trust Accounts is free to use and can be downloaded for free. The fees that you will see on the app are all paid to the miners or validators of their own respective blockchain. Trust Accounts does not collect or gain anything from these fees. Trust Accounts helps you manage your addresses, in order to send and receive Crypto. The app also provides the user the ability to do other Crypto transactions, like trading and staking.

Can I Buy Crypto with Trust Accounts?

Yes, we have Partner Providers that have integrated their services within the app to allow Trust Accounts users to Buy Cryptocurrencies with their credit cards. The app automatically sends your Crypto addresses to the providers depending on the Crypto you are trying to buy. No personal information is collected by the app. Your contact details and card information is sent to the crypto providers.

How do I Cash Out of Trust Accounts?

The app cannot convert your Crypto to Cash. What you can do is send the Crypto to an Exchange that allows Fiat conversion. Please do a Google Search to find the best way to convert your Crypto. These are your assets, this is your sole responsibility. Please manage them carefully.
